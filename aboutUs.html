About Us

We are a value-driven, customer-focused, creative and responsible fashion company. For us, fashion, fun and action are essential. We are defined by “the Fashonista way”: our culture, values and guidelines which reflect the heart and soul of Fashonista.

We are a value-driven, customer-focused, creative and responsible fashion company. For us, fashion, fun and action are essential. We are defined by “the H&M way”: our culture, values and guidelines which reflect the heart and soul of Fashonista.

“The Fashonista way” defines who we are, what we do and how we do it. It’s like a framework through which we can continuously manage our performance, work together and encourage new talents as they grow and develop.

We believe that our common values create energy and commitment. By providing a fun, creative and dynamic work place where teamwork is essential, we all grow together. Everyone working at Fashonista is an ambassador for our company – everything we do and say, internally and externally, matters.
